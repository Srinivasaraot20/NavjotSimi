🎯 Mock Test Strategy Tips

Maximize your mock test performance with these expert strategies from Dr. Navjot Simi IPS

Time Management

Practice with strict time limits. Aim to complete Prelims in 1.5 hours to have buffer time for review.

Smart Guessing

Use elimination technique. If you can eliminate 2 options, guessing becomes favorable due to negative marking.

Performance Analysis

Analyze every mock test. Focus on weak areas and track improvement over time.

Accuracy Focus

Maintain 65-70% accuracy in mocks. Quality over quantity - attempt only questions you're confident about.

Regular Practice

Take at least 2-3 mock tests per week. Consistency in practice leads to better performance.

Revision Strategy

Review incorrect answers immediately. Make notes of concepts you missed for quick revision.
